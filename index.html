Eden Hazard

Eden Hazard is a Belgian former professional footballer widely regarded as one of the most talented wingers of his generation. Known for his dribbling, creativity, and vision, Hazard began his career at Lille OSC, winning Ligue 1 before moving to Chelsea FC, where he won multiple Premier League and European trophies. He later joined Real Madrid, adding La Liga and other honors to his name. Hazard also captained Belgium to a third-place finish at the 2018 FIFA World Cup and is celebrated for his skill, sportsmanship, and impact on the game.
